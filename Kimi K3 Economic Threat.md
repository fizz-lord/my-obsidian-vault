# Kimi K3 Economic Threat Assessment

## What Happened
Chinese startup Moonshot released **Kimi K3**, a 2.8-trillion-parameter open-weight model reported to be roughly equal to Opus and GPT-5.6 Sol on benchmarks. Daniel Miessler argued this may represent a major economic risk to the United States because current US economic strength is partially built on frontier AI lab dominance.

## Why It Matters
- Open-weight parity removes US moat and broadens competitive attack surface overnight.
- Real-world reports showed Kimi K3 fixing security issues that US frontier models refused to address because of guardrails.
- US AI policy debate now has a concrete economic-exposure framing instead of only an arms-race framing.
